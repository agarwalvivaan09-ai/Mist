About MIST

Fragrance, made easier to carry.

MIST was created for a simple everyday problem: most perfume bottles are too large, fragile, or inconvenient to carry around.

The atomizer gives your existing fragrance a smaller format for school, work, gym bags, travel pouches, events, and gifting.

Our focus is practical design: a durable exterior, a clean refill process, and a look that feels minimal without feeling generic.

What we value

  • Useful products over novelty
  • Clear pricing and simple ordering
  • Compact design that still feels premium
  • Reliable support for damaged deliveries
