FAQ

Answers for refilling, daily use, delivery, compatibility, and replacements.

How do I refill it?

Remove the spray head of your perfume bottle, align the atomizer's bottom valve with the stem, and press down steadily until filled.

Will it work with every perfume bottle?

It works with most standard removable spray-stem perfume bottles. It may not work with sealed or unusual bottle designs.

Is it leak-proof?

It is designed to prevent leakage during normal use. Avoid overfilling and keep it away from heavy pressure inside bags.

How many sprays does it hold?

Approximately 30-35 sprays, depending on the fragrance and spray pressure.

What is the delivery time?

Delivery usually takes 5-7 working days after order confirmation.
