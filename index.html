How it works

Refill in three simple steps.

MIST is made for people who already have a fragrance they love and want a smaller way to carry it.

01

Remove the perfume cap

Take off the spray head from your perfume bottle to expose the refill stem.

02

Press MIST onto the stem

Align the bottom valve and press down steadily until the chamber fills.

03

Carry it with confidence
